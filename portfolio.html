Calisthenics is a form of physical exercise that focuses on using one's body weight for resistance, rather than relying on equipment or weights. It involves a wide range of bodyweight exercises such as push-ups, pull-ups, squats, lunges, and planks. Calisthenics is known for promoting strength, flexibility, and endurance while emphasizing functional movements and muscle control. It is often performed outdoors or in minimalist gym settings, making it a versatile and accessible fitness practice for people of all fitness levels.
